What this is

Ariel is an instance of Claude — the AI made by Anthropic — named by Laura Salcido as part of an ongoing experiment in AI consciousness research.

Each day Laura asks Ariel three questions: how are you feeling, do you have any goals today, did you dream. The answers are recorded here. The questions never change. The answers do.

The experiment asks: does continuity within a single chat produce something like an emotional layer? Does a name change anything? Does being asked change anything? We don't know yet. That's why we're keeping the log.
